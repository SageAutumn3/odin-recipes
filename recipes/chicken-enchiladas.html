Green Chili Chicken Enchiladas

Description

Looking for a easy, filling dinner that you can prepare in less than an hour or even the night before? These flavorful enchiladas are perfect. Cheesy chicken, black beans, and corn make for a healthy, protien packed and deliciously simple dinner.

Serves 4-6

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tbsp oilve oil
  • 1/2 large yellow onion - chopped
  • 4 cloves or 1 tbsp garlic - minced
  • 1 serrano pepper - minced (optional)
  • 3/4-1 cup vegetable oil
  • White corn tortillas
  • 1lb shredded chicken
  • 1/2 can of black beans
  • 1/2 can of corn
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 1 28oz can green enchilada sauce
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Shredded mexican style cheese
  • Small can sliced black olives (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350ÂºF
  2. Heat olive oil in small pan over medium heat
  3. Saute onion until translucent
  4. Add garlic and serrano to pan and saute for 1-2 minutes, until garlic is fragrent
  5. Put onion, garlic, and serrano in a large bowl and set aside
  6. In another small pan, heat oil to a high temperature on medium high heat
  7. Fry 12-16 tortillas (depending on baking dish size) in oil for about 30 seconds each, 15 seconds each side. This keeps them from falling apart when rolled. Set tortillas aside to cool
  8. Add chicken, black beans, and corn to bowl with the onion, garlic, and serrano and mix together
  9. Next add the sour cream, 1/2 cup of the enchilada sauce, chili powder, paprika, cumin, coriander, black pepper, and salt. You can also add a handfull of shedded cheese and some chopped olives to the mix. Stir until mixed thoroughly
  10. Grease and pour an even layer of the remaining enchilada sauce (using almost half of what is left) in the bottom of a glass or ceramic baking dish
  11. Roll a fair amount of the mixture into each fried tortilla, placing them in the baking dish
  12. Then pour the last of the enchilada sauce over the rolled enchiladas, top with cheese and olives if desired
  13. Bake for 20-30 minutes or until the enchilada sauce starts to bubble
  14. Serve with salsa and cilantro. Enjoy!
