Zucchini Black Bean Tacos

Description

Do you want tacos without all the grease and fat that meat brings? Try this vegetarian option with juicy zucchini, black beans, and corn. With the substance these ingredient bring, you won't even miss the meat.

Serves 4-6

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion - chopped
  • 4 cloves or 1 tbsp garlic - minced
  • 1 medium zucchini - chopped
  • 1/2 can black beans
  • 1/2 can corn
  • 1 tsp chili powder*
  • 1 tsp paprika*
  • 1/2 tsp cumin*
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Soft corn tortillas or taco shells
  • Avocado (optional)
  • Feta Cheese (optional)

*You can also substitute the seasonings with a packet of Taco Seasoning

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat
  2. Saute onion until translucent
  3. Add garlic and saute until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes
  4. Add chopped zucchini and stir until slightly cooked
  5. Add black beans and corn, then mix in chili powder, paprika, cumin, black pepper, and salt
  6. Stir until beans and corn are heated all the way through
  7. Heat tortillas or taco shells
  8. Top with avocado and feta cheese. Enjoy!
