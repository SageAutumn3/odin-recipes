Potato and Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Description

Broccoli Cheddar soup is always a crowd favorite, but with added potato it takes it to a whole new level. Thick and cheesy with crisp chuncks of broccoli florets, this soup is perfect for any chilly day.

Serves 4-6

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 yellow onion - chopped
  • 4 cloves or 1 tbsp garlic - minced
  • 4 cups or 1 carton chicken or vegetable stock
  • 3 large potatoes - cubed
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp ground mustard seed or substitute for 1/2 tsp yellow mustard
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 cups milk
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 3 cups shredded medium or mild cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. In a large pot melt butter over medium heat
  2. Add onion, saute until translucent
  3. Add garlic and saute 1-2 minutes or until fragrent
  4. Pour in chicken or vegetable stock and add paprika, ground mustard seed (or yellow mustard), black pepper, and salt
  5. Bring stock to a boil, then add cubed potatoes
  6. Cook potatoes until easily pierced with a fork, about 8-10 minutes
  7. Once potatoes are soft enough, blend with immersion blender, or mash, to desired consistency
  8. Pour in milk and add broccoli. Let simmer for about 8 minutes or until broccoli is al dente
  9. Toss shredded cheese and flour together
  10. Add cheese one cup at a time, stirring after each cup until cheese is completely melted
  11. Serve with a sprinkle of cheese and enjoy!
