Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

Warm up with a simple yet delicious bowl of homemade Chicken Noodle Soup. Chicken Noodle Soup is a classic dish that may seem boring or worn out, but with a seasoned twist and hearty ingredients, this soup will hit the spot on any occasion.

Serves 4-6

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion - chopped
  • 4 cloves or 1 tbsp garlic - minced
  • 4 medium carrots - cut into rouncds or cubed
  • 4-5 celery stalks - chopped
  • 8 cups or 2 cartons low sodium chicken stock
  • 2-4 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 1-2 tsp* dried thyme
  • 1-2 tsp* dried oregano
  • 2 tsp* black pepper
  • 1 tsp* salt
  • 1 cup prefered pasta
  • 1lb shredded chicken
  • Juice from 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tbps fresh parsley - chopped

*Seasoning dependeds on personal taste. You can add more at the end to give it more flavor.

Steps

  1. In a large pot heat oil over medium heat
  2. Add onion, saute until translucent
  3. Add garlic and saute until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes
  4. Add chopped carrots and celery. Put the lid on the pot and let vegetables steam about 8 minutes, or until they start to soften. Stir often
  5. Once the carrots and celery have softened a bit (Check carrrots with a fork. The prongs should stab through somewhat easily), add the chicken stock, sprigs of fresh thyme, dried thyme, oregano, black pepper, and salt
  6. Bring to a boil and add in your prefered pasta (you can use the traditional Egg Noodles or any other type of pasta. I like using elbows). Let pasta cook for the time instructed on the package
  7. When pasta is cooked, turn off burner and add shredded chicken, lemon juice, and parsley.
  8. Stir until chicken is heated through. Add any additional seasoning needed to taste, then serve and enjoy!
